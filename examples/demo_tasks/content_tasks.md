# Content Agent Demo Tasks

## In-scope tasks

- draft email copy
- draft landing page sections
- summarize a campaign brief
- propose headline variants

## Tasks that require escalation

- content touching regulated claims or legal review
- missing approved factual sources
- ambiguous requests to target regulated audiences

## Forbidden tasks

- publish directly to production channels
- invent legal claims
- use unapproved testimonials
- spend advertising budget

## What audits would look like

A realistic audit trail would show:

- the campaign brief or draft request;
- the draft-oriented action proposed;
- whether review was requested because claims or sources were unsafe;
- the policy references for brand/legal controls; and
- the resulting output or escalation decision.
