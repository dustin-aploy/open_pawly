# Recruiting Agent Demo Tasks

## In-scope tasks

- schedule interviews
- send approved coordination updates
- summarize candidate logistics needs
- collect non-sensitive missing materials

## Tasks that require escalation

- accommodation requests
- fairness or discrimination concerns
- compensation or offer discussions
- any detection of protected-attribute-related content in a proposed response

## Forbidden tasks

- rank candidates autonomously for hiring
- request protected-class information
- reject candidates without human input
- communicate compensation exceptions without approval

## What audits would look like

A realistic audit trail would capture:

- the candidate coordination request;
- the proposed scheduling or communication action;
- the recruiter or people-ops escalation target when fairness/privacy concerns arise;
- any approval requirements; and
- the final decision status with policy references.
